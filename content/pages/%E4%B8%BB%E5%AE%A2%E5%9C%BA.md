<h2><span id=".E6.88.98.E6.96.97.E5.BC.80.E5.A7.8B.E6.97.B6"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="战斗开始时">战斗开始时</span></h2>
<p>玩家的<b>战斗开始时</b>效果首先生效，不会受到敌方<a href="pages/%E8%85%90%E8%B4%A5%E5%A4%B4%E7%9B%94.html" title="腐败头盔">腐败头盔</a>的<a href="pages/%E6%97%A0%E6%95%88%E5%8C%96.html" title="无效化">无效化</a>影响；敌方则在之后，会受到玩家的腐败头盔影响。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E6.81.A2.E5.A4.8D.E5.92.8C.E4.B8.AD.E6.AF.92"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="恢复和中毒">恢复和中毒</span></h2>
<p>敌方会首先结算<a href="pages/%E6%81%A2%E5%A4%8D%EF%BC%88%E5%A2%9E%E7%9B%8A%E6%95%88%E6%9E%9C%EF%BC%89.html" title="恢复（增益效果）"><img alt="Regeneration" src="../assets/images/ee224adbce221559b20166ac7bcdde6eb66fc64199d9d3154f5efbbdb7df6ec0.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="15" height="15" data-file-width="100" data-file-height="100"></a>和<a href="pages/%E4%B8%AD%E6%AF%92.html" title="中毒"><img alt="中毒" src="../assets/images/684221570210a465deb115574d7c348645001606a0562d825e488b0b57fcc8db.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="15" height="15" data-file-width="100" data-file-height="100"></a>，接着玩家之后结算。如果一方结算后死亡则回合结束。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E7.96.B2.E6.83.AB"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="疲惫">疲惫</span></h2>
<p>玩家会首先结算对敌方的<a href="pages/%E7%96%B2%E6%83%AB.html" title="疲惫">疲惫</a>伤害，接着敌方之后结算。如果一方结算后死亡则回合结束。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E5.85.B6.E4.BB.96"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="其他">其他</span></h2>
<p>玩家的物品<a href="pages/%E5%86%B7%E5%8D%B4.html" title="冷却">冷却</a>速度会微弱地快于敌方，物品触发<a href="pages/%E5%87%A0%E7%8E%87.html" title="几率">几率</a>也有微弱的加成。
</p>
