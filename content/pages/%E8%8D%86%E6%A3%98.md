**尖刺**是一种[增益效果](pages/%E5%A2%9E%E7%9B%8A%E6%95%88%E6%9E%9C.html)。当被敌人用[![Image 2: 近战](../assets/images/7e1844ca83fb494317db372c6aee6df1fe281916bce37fc1f19213415df44f7c.png)](pages/%E8%BF%91%E6%88%98.html "近战")武器击中时，每层造成1点伤害。（最高造成100%受到伤害的量）

[自然护符](pages/%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6%E6%8A%A4%E7%AC%A6.html)和[尖刺墙](pages/%E5%B0%96%E5%88%BA%E5%A2%99.html)可以提高[![Image 3: 近战](../assets/images/7e1844ca83fb494317db372c6aee6df1fe281916bce37fc1f19213415df44f7c.png)](pages/%E8%BF%91%E6%88%98.html "近战")造成的最高尖刺伤害量并使其对[![Image 4: 远程](../assets/images/157250e4fda9aea26ae09e11886c23c0211d75a337fb1cdfa7d1e10f882eb833.png)](pages/%E8%BF%9C%E7%A8%8B.html "远程")也造成尖刺伤害。例单个自然护符：最高造成150%[![Image 5: 近战](../assets/images/7e1844ca83fb494317db372c6aee6df1fe281916bce37fc1f19213415df44f7c.png)](pages/%E8%BF%91%E6%88%98.html "近战")伤害量的尖刺伤害，最高造成50%[![Image 6: 远程](../assets/images/157250e4fda9aea26ae09e11886c23c0211d75a337fb1cdfa7d1e10f882eb833.png)](pages/%E8%BF%9C%E7%A8%8B.html "远程")伤害量的尖刺伤害。
