<li><a href="pages/%E5%85%89%E4%B9%8B%E9%BB%8F%E9%BB%8F.html" title="光之黏黏">光之黏黏</a>：致盲5→6</li>

<li><a href="pages/%E8%85%90%E8%B4%A5%E6%B0%B4%E6%99%B6.html" title="腐败水晶">腐败水晶</a>（物品栏）：冷却时间5秒→4.5秒</li>
